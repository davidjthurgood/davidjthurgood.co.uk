Process.

I take a holistic approach to my work. This helps me understand and create solutions to the task in hand. My process consists of three main stages;

  • Definition; Distill the brief, breaking it down into various tasks in an effort to understand the problems and produce a range of solutions.
  • Visualisation; Production of low or high fidelity mockups and prototypes, using best UX and design practices.
  • Validation; Gathering feedback from stakeholders alongside user research to inform the next iteration of the project.

Cycling through this process allows me to create extensively tested solutions that meet the business and user needs. Once an idea has been validated I take it through to production.
