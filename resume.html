Process.

Process is important to my craft, I try to take a holistic approach to the projects I undertake. Consisting of three main stages this helps me understand and create solutions to the task in hand.

  • Definition; Distill the brief, understand the problem and produce a solution
  • Visualisation; Production of low or high fidelity mockups and prototypes
  • Validation; Gathering feedback from stakeholders alongside user testing
